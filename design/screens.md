# Screen Specifications & Data

This document contains high-fidelity specs and realistic data snippets for major app screens.

1) Onboarding — Cinematic flow
- Frames: mobile 390x844 (iPhone 14). 3 slides: hero, features, personalization.
- Elements: large headline, floating glass cards, animated particle/gradient backdrop.
- Sample copy: "Meet Klak — your AI finance partner. Smarter than your bank."

2) Authentication
- Centered auth forms, frosted card background, minimal chrome.
- Fields: phone (with Nigerian format), password, biometric prompt.
- Sample user: +234 812 345 6789

3) Dashboard (Hero)
- Dominant BalanceCard with total balance, count-up animation, actionable quick pills.
- Secondary panels: AI Financial Score (78), Spending Trend (last 30 days), Monthly Savings (₦34,200).
- Sample data:
  - Total balance: ₦1,243,520.00
  - AI Score: 78 (Confidence: 92%)
  - Monthly spending: ₦324,450 ↓ 8% vs last month

4) AI Insights
- Feed of `InsightCard`s with predictions and impact bars.
- Example insight: "Reduce dining-out by 15% to save ₦12,400/month — projected to increase AI Score +3."

5) Budgets
- Interactive rings per category, live progress, ability to reassign funds.
- Example categories: Groceries (₦45,000/₦50,000), Transport (₦12,000/₦20,000), Subscriptions (₦6,400/₦10,000)

6) Transactions
- Fast-scrolling list, merchant logos, smart tags (Auto categorize). Expandable detail with timeline of reconciliation.

7) Subscription / Pricing
- Animated comparison table, highlight best plan in gold, monthly vs annual toggle.

8) Profile & Settings
- Minimal settings with large affordances for biometric, security, connected accounts.
